## extract_and_save_recipe

Extract a recipe from a cookbook photo using AI vision and save it to the recipe catalogue. The photo from the current conversation is used automatically — just provide the cookbook name if mentioned.

## search_recipes

Search the recipe catalogue by name, cookbook, or tags. Returns matching recipes with name, cookbook, tags, prep/cook time, and usage count.

## get_recipe_details

Get full recipe details including ingredients list, step-by-step instructions, photo URL, and all metadata.

## recipe_to_grocery_list

Generate a grocery list from a saved recipe. Cross-references ingredients against grocery purchase history to show what's needed vs what you likely already have. Supports servings scaling.

## list_cookbooks

List all saved cookbooks with their recipe counts. Use to show what's been catalogued.

## search_downshiftology

Search Downshiftology.com for healthy recipes by course, cuisine, ingredient, dietary preference, or time constraint. Returns a numbered list of matching recipes with names, times, tags, and links.

## get_downshiftology_details

Get full details of a Downshiftology recipe from search results, including ingredients, instructions, nutrition, and grocery history match.

## import_downshiftology_recipe

Import a Downshiftology recipe into the family's recipe catalogue for meal planning. Checks for duplicates by source URL.
